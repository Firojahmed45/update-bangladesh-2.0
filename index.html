Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the practical implementation of ideas that result in the introduction of new goods or services.

Sustainability

Sustainability can be defined as the capacity to maintain or improve the state and availability of desirable materials.

Infrastructure
